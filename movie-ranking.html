Calvin's Greatest Movies of All Time

Below are a list of the best movies I have seen...According to me

All Dogs Go To Heaven

Animated film about a dog who has died but is sent back to Earth to prove that he can be a "Good Dog" and enter Heaven. This is a childhood classic!

The Adjustment Bureau

This movie gives a uncanny look into the decisions we make both small and big and hoe they can have rippiling effects in both a personal lives and our environment. This movie really makes you think about your actions and the effects they may have

World War Z

Best interpretation of a zombie outbreak I have ever seen.

Deadpool

Marvel anti-hero turned comedian, one of the best action comedies ever!
